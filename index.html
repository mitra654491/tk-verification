Welcome to ingooglesheets App

This application allows users to upload videos from Google Drive to their TikTok accounts via the TikTok API. The app authenticates users with TikTok OAuth 2.0, retrieves videos from Google Drive, and publishes them directly to the user's TikTok account, either immediately or as a scheduled post. The application requires permissions for video upload and publishing.
